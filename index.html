Union Budget's Historic ₹11.11 Lakh Crore Allocation to Propel Infrastructure Growth

The Union Budget 2024, presented by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman, allocates a record ₹11.11 lakh crore to infrastructure, marking 3.4% of GDP. Key highlights include ₹15,000 crore for Andhra Pradesh, ₹26,000 crore for Bihar's expressways and industrial hubs, and ₹11,500 crore for Bihar's irrigation and flood control. The budget also supports the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) Phase IV to connect 25,000 rural areas, alongside flood and landslide management funding for Assam, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and Sikkim. Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman has announced a ₹2.2 lakh crore initiative to improve urban housing for one crore poor and middle-class families, as part of a ₹10 lakh crore allocation.
